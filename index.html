About the Conference

Ireland's Premier
Housing Forum

Each year, the Housing Practitioners' Conference brings together local authorities, approved housing bodies, government departments, and housing professionals from across Ireland to share expertise, tackle shared challenges, and drive meaningful progress.

The 2026 conference focuses on the collective action needed to realise Ireland's National Housing Plan — with dedicated sessions on delivery, reform, supply, and the future of housing design.

Ministerial Addresses

Direct engagement with James Browne TD, Minister for Housing, and Kieran O'Donnell, Minister of State, on national housing policy.

Expert-Led Workshops

12 practitioner workshops covering vacancy, AHB delivery, disability housing, AI, tenancy sustainment, and more.

Housing Visits & Clinics

On-the-ground visits to Wexford housing schemes and one-to-one clinics with Housing Agency subject matter experts.

National Networking

Connect with peers from every corner of the housing sector — from Fingal to Cork, from AHBs to central government.
